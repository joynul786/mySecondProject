Description

Accent is a modern and sophisticated free web design studio website template. Accent makes sure to deliver all your content stunningly with a clean and minimalistic approach to the appearance. Regardless of the device and platform they use, your website will look all the time seamlessly and provide a first-grade experience for all your visitors. Accent is also a fantastic resource for agencies, freelancers, and small businesses. Besides, Accent sports a one-page layout which ensures all the details and information are just a few scrolls away.

The bundle of goodies includes a full-screen slider, services section, hover effects, sticky details, and a working contact form. Accent also features a newsletter subscription widget, social media icons, sticky header, and testimonials. The default layout already rocks a flexible and fluid design that is in tune with web browsers and retina screens.
